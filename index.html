Spectacular Views, European Style, and Peaceful Seclusion

Simple old world elegance, panoramic lake views, and peaceful privacy await you at Loch Haven's mountainside paradise. Breathtaking views of Clear Lake and the feeling of being away from it all, if only for a few days.

Enjoy the spectacular views of Clear Lake and the surrounding hills while you have coffee & breakfast on the terrace, or share a romantic glass of wine from the bottle you just picked up at one of the many local vineyards in the evening. Pick your own fruit and vegetables from our garden (seasonal) while birds swirl overhead. Kick back in the rocker and read a great book in the soothing silence. Take a tranquil stretch on the front porch.
